Accessibility Statement

TAI TCM GmbH is committed to making its website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of individual ability or technical limitations.

Compliance status

This website is designed with reference to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1, Level AA) published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

We continuously work to improve accessibility and to reduce potential barriers step by step.

Measures already implemented

  • Clear page structure and easy-to-understand navigation
  • Sufficient color contrast for improved readability
  • Responsive design for mobile devices
  • Basic usability without a mouse (keyboard navigation)

Known limitations

Despite our efforts, some content or functions may not yet be fully accessible. In particular, we have limited control over the accessibility of external services embedded on this website (e.g., Google Maps or online appointment booking).
